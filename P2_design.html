Brief discussion

As we are aware of that the name of our site may be mistaken for something else than a romance gaming review site, we chose a slogan to “correct” this misunderstanding with “Our reviews, your match”. With this slogan, where we specifically mention “Reviews” we believe it will be almost impossible for you to misunderstand after reading it. This logo and slogan will be on every page of our GeiMate site.
When you press “Categories” you won’t be directed to a new website, unless you press either “Dating Sim”, “RPG” or Visual Novel. Compared to “About us” however, if you press “About us” you will be directed to “Contact Information” as a default.
We have chosen rose, pink colors for the navigation bar to support our theme of a romance gaming review site. Since the navigation bar background is pink, we chose a white text color, as a black text color would seem too harsh.
We figured having “shadow” to the content background would make it pop a lot more and separate it from the grey background. This fulfills our goal of making the subtle, pink content background more eye-catching.
Links of the site, will have the same basic colors as the navigation bar, with a rose-pink color. There will also be a difference in the hovering. This is so that we can keep our “romantic” theme, but also so that the design corresponds with what a simple user would expect from links.
Content text is a subtle grey, instead of a harsh black, which we believe would be very contradicting with our pink and subtle theme.
We will have titles in the content section a bit darker grey (but still grey and not black) and a bit bigger than the normal paragraph size to separate them.
We believe our universal design decisions fulfill our goals of a “romance”-theme gaming review site and audience.
