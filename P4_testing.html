Testing document

The user testing section is a brief summary of the questionnaire we sent out to testers who would to test the website, but were not available for a live usertest.
The Live User Testing section consist of the actions the testers had to perform and what observation we made from them. After taking the test, the Live testers gave feedback as listed in the tables below, and also had to complete the Google form.

User testing

Our Google Form includes the four main sections:

  1. General infromation:
    This section asked for the platform type, browsertype, age, gender and profession.
  2. Usefulness:
    This section questioned the user about how useful she or he felt the website was, if the website met the testers needs, and if it did everything the tester was expecting it to do.
  3. Ease of use:
    This section asked about the use, if it was easy for the user to navigate thorugh the website, if using ti was effortless and if the tester didn't notice any inconsistencies while using it.
  4. Ease of learning:
    The ease of Learning is questioning about how long it took for the user to learn how to use it.
  5. Satisfaction:
    The satisfication section is asking about how satisfied the testers are with the design and the way you navigate.
Link to the Google form: Open Google Form.

Feedback Summary

After reading though the responses from our usertest we found out that our user were very happy with the usefullness of the site. They did however have some problems with the search. They thougt that it was confusing that the search was on sites that the search did not work. And some of them tought that it was confusing that the pictures did not link.
There where no new complaints about the "ease of use". We got a good score from the test. Same for "ease of learning", and "satisfaction". Only bad score we got was from our users that uses a mac computer, they thought that the site was not pleasant to use.

Our response to the feedback

We looked at the feedback we got from the users and changed realized that some of the users where testing an old version of the site. After finding our testsubjects again and showing them the improved site they were happy that we had thought of their complaints.
